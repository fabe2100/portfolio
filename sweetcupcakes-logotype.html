This logotype was designed specifically for the Sweet Cupcakes bakery project. It focuses on creating a unique and memorable brand identity that reflects the bakery's charm and appeal. The design was crafted using Affinity Designer, emphasizing clean typography and a warm, inviting aesthetic.

The logotype is used across various branding materials including packaging, signage, and digital media to establish a consistent and professional visual presence.
