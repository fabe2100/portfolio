Etoile Restaurant Logotype

This logotype was created for the Etoile restaurant to reflect its elegant and modern brand identity. The design was crafted with attention to detail using Affinity Designer, focusing on clean lines, balanced typography, and a sophisticated color palette.

The logotype serves as a key visual element for the restaurant's branding, used across various media including signage, menus, and digital platforms.
