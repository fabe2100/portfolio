About Me

Passionate UX/UI & Graphic Designer

I'm Fauzia Ben Sliman, an engaged and ambitious UX/UI and graphic designer with a broad educational background in digital design. I am passionate about creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing digital solutions, always eager to explore new techniques and design methods.

My skills include user experience, visual communication, interactive design, web development, and responsive design. I have experience with tools like Figma, Affinity Designer, and WordPress, and a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

I combine creativity with a user-centered approach, focusing on accessibility and meaningful digital experiences.
