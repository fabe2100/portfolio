Healthy Flyer Project - Advertising Poster

  • Advertising
  • Original Artwork
  • Typography
  • Layout
  • Health Awareness
  • Print Design

This project involved creating a unique advertising flyer using original materials such as own photographs, illustrations, and logos. The flyer aims to promote health awareness through clear and effective visual communication.

The design focuses on using typography, layout, and color to attract attention and convey the advertising message clearly to the audience.

Reflection

In the project documentation, I discuss the choices made in the flyer design, including the advertising goal, the sender's identity, and how the design elements support the message. The reflection connects these decisions to relevant course literature with proper Harvard referencing.

This project provided valuable experience in creating original advertising materials that effectively communicate a message through thoughtful design.
