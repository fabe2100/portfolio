Yummy Scents: Visual Identity Project

  • Responsive Design
  • E-commerce Webstore
  • Gourmand Perfumes
  • Natural Ingredients
  • User-friendly Experience
  • Multi-device Optimization

The visual identity project for Yummy Scents focused on creating a distinctive and cohesive brand image that captures the essence of gourmand perfumes with natural ingredients. The work involved designing a custom logo, selecting a harmonious color palette, and choosing typography that conveys elegance and approachability. The visual elements were crafted to ensure consistency across packaging, digital platforms, and marketing materials, enhancing brand recognition and customer engagement.
